Hi 👋, I’m Abhinav. I am a PhD scholar at IIIT Hyderabad, working at LTRC. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, and multilingual reasoning, particularly in Indian languages.

Research Interests:

  • Multilingual reasoning in large language models
  • Low-resource and Indic NLP
  • Machine translation for Indian languages

I am interested in understanding how LLMs reason in multilingual settings and how culturally grounded knowledge can improve performance on low-resource languages.
